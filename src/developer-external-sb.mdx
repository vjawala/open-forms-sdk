# Embedding in external storybook

Users of [NL Design System](https://www.nldesignsystem.nl/) are encouraged to incorporate our custom
(Formio) components into their own Storybook for visual regression testing. We provide the React
package/component
[`@open-formulieren/formio-renderer`](https://github.com/open-formulieren/formio-renderer) that you
should be able to incorporate pretty easily.

Here you find guidelines on _how_ to achieve this and the necessary hoops to jump through.
